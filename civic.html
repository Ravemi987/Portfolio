At ENSEEIHT, we must complete approximately 30 hours of civic engagement ("engagement RSE" in French) before the end of the 9th semester. This engagement aims to make an engineer a responsible citizen, concerned about societal, environmental, and ethical issues of their future professional path. It can be validated in many ways. In fact, our school accepts almost every concrete or consequent project.

My admission to the school in the second year of the curriculum demanded quite an important period of academic adaptation. Therefore, this engagement is not really concretized yet. However, I actively draw up ideas and plans. For instance, I will combine my passion and expertise around three potential areas of commitment, each including a social or entrepreneurial dimension, which will prepare me for the corporate environment.

Social Impact Project

Chess Analysis Engine

I am currently working on a chess engine application allowing players to analyze games in real-time, with a high level of reliability. It aims to break down financial barriers, as connected DGT boards are very expensive, while improving the gaming experience. In addition, most mobile programs implementing detection show many flaws. This application could be finally delivered to clubs or YouTube influencers.
